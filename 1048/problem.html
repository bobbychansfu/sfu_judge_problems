Dogwarts Academy

Dogwarts is an open education school of magic for those who want to avoid dealing with the high tuition fees and internal politics of Hogwarts. To make things simple, they have decided to sort students based on four abilities: dark arts, vision, mind reader, and enchantment. All students are labelled with a positive number. The following table describes the students abilities based on their student numbers.

student number ability
divisible by 3 dark arts
divisible by 4 vision
divisible by 5 mind reader
divisible by 7 enchantment
note that you can have students in all four categories or none of the categories.

Write a student curation program that collects students' numbers until a negative number is entered, then reports the number of students in each category.

Input

Prompt the user for student numbers until a negative number is entered.

Output

Output the number of students with each ability.
