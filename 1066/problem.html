Pizza Time!

Bobby wants to order pizza for dinner but can't decide which option is the better deal. There are 2 pizza options available. Help Bobby determine which pizza has the lower price per square centimeter.

The program should take as input:

  • Diameter and price of pizza 1
  • Diameter and price of pizza 2
Then, output which pizza is the better buy based on the lower price per sq cm. Use the formula for area of a circle to calculate the area from the diameter.

Sample Run 1

Here is a sample run of your program. Please note that the bold text represents user inputs. Please note that the prompts must match the following exactly to get maximum points. 

Enter the diameter of pizza 1: 13.4 
Enter the price of pizza 1: 5.00 
Enter the diameter of pizza 2: 29.4 
Enter the price of pizza 2: 17.34 
The second pizza is the better buy.
