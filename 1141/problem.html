Nubby's Number Farm

The owners of Nubby's Number Factory were amazed by how well it worked and all the HUGE numbers it created! But they are ambitious, and decided to make Nubby the mascot of their new number farm as well!

The digit plants have grown, so now it's your turn to use Nubby to harvest them. The plants have all been grown in a row, and each digit plant has a digit from $1$ to $9$. When using Nubby to harvest a plant, however, he destroys both plants next to it.

After you harvest the plants, make sure to place them side by side to make as large of a number as you can!

Input

The first line will contain an integer $n$, the number of plants. The second line will contain a string of $n$ digits, each a number between $1$ and $9$.

Output

Print the largest number that you can make by harvesting plants with Nubby and putting them in any order you choose.

Sample Input 1

3
456

Sample Output 1

64

Sample Input 2

6
961488

Sample Output 2

984

Scoring

50 points: $n$ is odd, $n \leq 100$

75 points: $n$ can be even, $n \leq 1000$

100 points: $n \leq 10^5$
