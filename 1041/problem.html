Year of the Rabbit

Toki Loves the year of the rabbit. Each year, he looks forward to the next time the year of the rabbit comes again. Help Toki to write a program to help him calculate the number of years until the year of the rabbit comes again! Prompt the user to enter a year $n$, then output $t$, the number of years until the next year of the rabbit.

The year of the rabbit happens every twelve years away from 2023. For example, ... 1999, 2011, 2023, 2035, ...

Input

An integer $n$ representing the current year.

Output

The number $t$, the number of years until the next year of the rabbit.
