Harry Salary

Harry is excited to start his new job. His boss tells him that he will be given $n$ cents the first day, and his salary will double each day. Help Harry writes a program to calculate his total income after $d$ days in dollars.

Input

Prompt the user for integer $d$, the number days and $n$ the number of cents paid the first day.

Output

The total salary after $d$ days.

Sample Run 1

Here is a sample run of your program. Please note that the bold text represents user inputs. The prompts must match exactly to get a full score. 

d: 4
n: 5
0.75

Explanation:
Harry gets 5 cents on the first day, 10 cents on the second day, 20 cents on the third, 40 cents on the fourth, for a total of 75 cents.
