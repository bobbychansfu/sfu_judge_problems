Bob's Cubes

Bob loves Rubik's cubes of all sizes. Given that he has a cube with each edge measuring length a, he would like to know the cube's surface area and volume.

A cube has 6 sides, and the formulae for the area and volume are:

Surface Area = $6a^2$
Volume = $a^3$

Input

One line with an integer $a$ denoting the side-length of the cube. Do not prompt the user in this question.

Output

2 space-separated integers denoting the surface area and volume of the cube respectively.

Constraints

$0 \le n \le 100$

Sample Input

8

Sample Output

384 512
