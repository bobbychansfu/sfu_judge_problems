Summit Peak

Alice loves climbing mountains. She usually celebrates her climb by putting a flag at the summit. Help Alice to write a program that draws a picture of her climb based on the height of the mountain.

Input

The input will consist of a single integer $n$ ($1 \leq n \leq 20$), the height of the mountain. There are no prompts for this question.

Output

Output the mountain and flag, as in the samples. Please be careful with whitespace, as the solution is sensitive to whitespace used.
