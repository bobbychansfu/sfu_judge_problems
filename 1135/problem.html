Afu's Saving Plan

Afu Miyan has set a savings goal and is determined to save money every week until the goal is reached. However, the amount saved each week may vary. Afu needs a Python program to help track these varying weekly savings and determine how long it will take to reach the goal.

You are to write a Python program that:

  1. Prompts the user to input their total savings goal.
  2. Continuously asks the user for their savings amount for each week until the savings goal is reached.
  3. Calculates the total savings and the number of weeks it took to reach or exceed the savings goal.
  4. Once the goal is reached or exceeded, prints a congratulatory message along with the total number of weeks it took to reach the goal.

Input

An integer $G$ representing the total savings goal.
A series of integers representing the weekly savings amount. The series of integers will continue until the total savings goal is reached or exceeded.

Output

A congratulatory message stating the total number of weeks it took to reach the savings goal.
