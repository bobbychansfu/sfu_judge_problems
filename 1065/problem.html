Snape's Ingredients

Dr. Snape (Professor of Potions) at Hogwarts aims to preplan his potion classes. For each different class, he needs to use different potions and herbs:

For classes before 9am, he is teaching Polyjuice Potion which requires:

  • Boomslang skin
  • Hair of the person you want to turn into
  • Horn of bicorn
  • Leeches

For the 9am class, he is teaching the Luck Potion Felix Felicis which requires:

  • Occamy eggshell
  • Ashwinder egg
  • Dash of tincture of thyme
  • Goldfish

For classes after 9am, he is teaching nothing but remind him to go smack Mr. Potter.

Write a program that takes in the class time as input and outputs the ingredients Professor Snape needs for that class.
