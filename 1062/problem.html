Moving Day

In Korea, there are many myths around moving on certain lunar calendar dates. Specifically, people tend to avoid moving into new places on dates ending in 9 or 0 in the lunar calendar. For example, January 9th on the lunar calendar would be considered an unfavorable move-in date.

Additionally, moving in winter (defined as November through January) is not ideal, as it is more difficult to inspect the condition of a new home in cold weather.

Please write a program that:

  • Prompts the user to input a desired move-in date in the format "MM DD" (e.g. "11 1" for November 1st)
  • Checks if the input date falls on a lunar date ending in 9 or 0
  • Checks if the input date falls within November, December, or January
  • Prints output stating whether the input date is considered a good or bad day to move in, based on the lunar date and winter season factors.

Input

One line with the month $m$ and day $d$ separated by a space.

Output

One line stating "It is a good day to move in" or "It is not a good day to move in".

Constraints

$1 \le m \le 12$
$1 \le d \le 31$
