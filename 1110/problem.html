Ingredients List

A wise old chef is preparing for a grand feast. She has a list of grocery items to buy, each with a different price. Your task is to help her sort this list from the most expensive item to the least expensive, so she can manage her budget effectively.

Assist the wise chef in organizing her grocery list. You'll receive an integer $n$, indicating the number of items she needs. Following this, $n$ lines will list each item along with its price. Your goal is to rearrange this list, starting with the priciest item down to the cheapest. Conclude by calculating the total cost of all items.

Input Format

A single integer $n$, the number of ingredients
The next $n$ lines will contain the name of the ingredient, followed by a space, followed by the price of the ingredient (an integer).

Output

output the list of ingredients in descending order of price, followed by the total cost of all ingredients.

Constraints

  • $1 \leq n \leq 1000$

Sample Input 1

3
Apples 5
Bread 2
Cheese 8

Sample Output 1

Cheese 8
Apples 5
Bread 2
15
