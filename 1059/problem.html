Jojo's Simple Interest

Jojo is taking out a loan of $P$ dollars from the bank at an interest rate of $r%$ per year. He had to pay back the loan and all accrued interest after $t$ years.

Write a program to help Jojo calculate and print the total amount (principal + interest) Jojo had to pay back after $t$ years.

The formula for simple interest is:
$A = P(1 + rt)$
Where, $A$ = Total amount of Interest
$P$ = Principal amount
$r$ = Rate of interest per year in decimal
$t$ = Time period in years

Input

The values $A$, $P$, $r$, and $t$. Please note you do not need to prompt the user in this question.

Output

The total amount that Jojo needs to pay back rounded to $2$ decimal places.

Constraints

$0 \le P \le 100000$
$0 \le r \le 20$
$0 \le t \le 20$

Sample Input

123.45
0.05
3

Sample Output

141.97
