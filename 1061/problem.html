B-Day

The current year is 2023 and it's your friend's birthday. You know his or her name and the year of his or her birth. Write a program to remind your friend of their current age.

Input

One line with a string denoting your friend's name. Then another line containing an integer $y$, the year he or she was born. Do not prompt the user in this question.

Output

Wish your friend happy birthday. Then print out their current age.

Constraints

$1980 \le y \le 2023$

Sample Input

Jared
1996

Sample Output

Happy Birthday Jared! Your current age is 27
