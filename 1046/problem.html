Happy New Year

Sara is practicing her countdown for the new years parade. She needs help counting from an arbitrary number $n$ by steps of $t$ down to $0$. Write a program to help Sara do this.

Input

Prompt the user to enter $n$, the strating number, and the steps $t$.

Output

Output all the numbers from $n$ (inclusive) to 0 (exclusive) in steps sizes of $t$, finishing with "Happy New Year!"
