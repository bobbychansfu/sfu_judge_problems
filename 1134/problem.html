Prime Number Game

John and Mia love playing number games. They have recently come up with a new game that involves prime factors. Here's how the game works:

  1. Each player chooses a number.
  2. They find the prime factors of their chosen numbers.
  3. They sum up the prime factors (counting repeated factors as many times as they occur).
  4. If the sums of the prime factors of their numbers are equal, they win. Otherwise, they lose.
Your task is to write a program that determines whether John and Mia win or lose the game based on the numbers they choose.

Input

The first line contains two integers $A$ and $B$, where $1 \leq A, B \leq 10^6$ representing the numbers chosen by John and Mia, respectively.

Output

Print "Win" if John and Mia win the game (i.e., the sums of the prime factors of $A$ and $B$ are equal), and "Lose" otherwise.

Sample Run 1

Here is a sample run of your program. Please note that the bold text represents user inputs. Please note there are no prompts. 

10 15 
Lose

Sample Run 2 

9 8
Win
